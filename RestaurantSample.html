Fong Seng Nasi Lemak

Address: 22 Clementi Rd, Singapore 129751

Opening Hours: 6am-4am (closed on Tuesdays)

Phone: +65 9092 1651

An institution in itself, Fong Seng Nasi Lemak in Clementi is a godsend for night owls as it operates till 4.00AM every day. They offer the standard coconut-infused rice as well as trays upon trays of fried and saucy ingredients. Think, your standard fried fish, chicken wings, otah, ikan bilis, fried egg as well as slightly more unconventional options such as luncheon meat, hash browns, chicken cutlet, fish cake, teriyaki saba fish and more.

To better accommodate students and to keep one step ahead of their competitors, they maintain relatively affordable prices!
